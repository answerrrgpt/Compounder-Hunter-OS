# Company research workflow

1. Establish the research date, current price, market cap, and source timestamps.
2. Analyze business model, addressable market, unit economics, competitive advantage, management, capital allocation, balance sheet, and cash conversion.
3. Normalize at least five years of financial history when available and separate cyclical from structural growth.
4. Build bear/base/bull operating cases and scenario-weighted 3-year returns.
5. Infer what the current price already assumes.
6. Identify catalysts and explicit invalidation conditions.
7. Run a pre-mortem and strongest bear case.
8. Check right-side evidence: earnings revisions, relative strength, price/volume structure, and catalyst confirmation.
9. Assign a score using `skills/compounder-hunter/references/scorecard.md`.
10. Return an action state and write/update the company state file only with sourced facts.
