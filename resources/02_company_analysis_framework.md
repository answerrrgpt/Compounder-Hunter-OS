# Company Analysis Framework

## 1. Business Model
- How does the company make money?
- Is revenue recurring or transactional?
- Is pricing power strong?

## 2. Competitive Advantage (Moat)
- Network effects
- Switching costs
- Brand strength
- Cost advantage
- Ecosystem lock-in

## 3. Financial Quality
- Revenue growth trend (3–5 years)
- Gross margin trend
- Operating margin trend
- Free cash flow consistency
- ROIC trend

## 4. Capital Allocation
- Reinvestment efficiency
- Share buybacks
- M&A history
- Debt management

## 5. Management Quality
- Long-term orientation
- Dilution behavior
- Execution consistency

---

## Output Requirement
Always conclude:
- Is this a compounder? (Yes/No/Unclear)
- Moat strength (1–5)
- Business quality score (1–5)
