# Investment Checklist

Before buying any stock:

## Business
- [ ] Understand revenue model
- [ ] Understand customers

## Moat
- [ ] Clear competitive advantage
- [ ] Durable over 5+ years

## Financials
- [ ] Positive FCF
- [ ] Strong ROIC

## Valuation
- [ ] Not in bubble zone
- [ ] Reasonable upside

## Trend
- [ ] Not in free fall
- [ ] Signs of stabilization

## Risk
- [ ] No existential risk
