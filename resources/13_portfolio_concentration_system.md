# Portfolio Concentration System (V3)

## Core Philosophy

This is a HIGH-CONVICTION, CONCENTRATED portfolio system.

Goal:
Maximize 3–5 year compounding returns by concentrating capital into the best 3–8 ideas.

---

## Position Structure

### Core Holdings (70–90%)

- 3 to 6 companies maximum
- Long-term compounders
- Held for 3–5 years

Criteria:
- Strong moat
- High ROIC
- Large TAM
- Confirmed trend or early right-side base

---

### Satellite Positions (10–30%)

- 1–3 positions
- Higher uncertainty but high upside
- Catalyst-driven

---

## Hard Rules

- Maximum holdings: 8 total
- Top 1 position ≤ 35%
- Top 3 positions ≥ 60% combined
- No “diworsification” (over-diversification)

---

## Capital Allocation Logic

Increase position when:

- Trend confirms (right-side entry)
- Catalyst appears
- Fundamentals accelerate

Reduce position when:

- Thesis weakening
- Trend breaks
- Moat erosion

---

## Key Principle

We do NOT optimize for safety through diversification.

We optimize for asymmetric upside.
