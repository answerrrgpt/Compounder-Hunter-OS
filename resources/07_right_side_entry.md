# Right Side Entry System

## Objective
Avoid catching falling knives. Enter only when trend stabilizes or reverses upward.

---

## Entry Conditions

- Weekly trend stabilizing
- Monthly trend turning upward
- Volume confirmation
- Fundamentals stable or improving
- No negative earnings revision cycle

---

## Entry Stages

### Stage A: Observation
Downtrend, no action

### Stage B: Stabilization
Base formation

### Stage C: Breakout (Preferred Entry)
Trend reversal confirmed

---

## Key Principle
We do not predict bottoms. We confirm trends.
