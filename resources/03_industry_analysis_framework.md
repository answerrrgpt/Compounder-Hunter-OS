# Industry Analysis Framework

## 1. Industry Lifecycle
- Early stage / Growth / Mature / Decline

## 2. Market Size (TAM)
- Current size
- 5–10 year expansion potential

## 3. Competitive Structure
- Fragmented or consolidated?
- Winner-takes-all dynamics?

## 4. Key Drivers
- Technology shift
- Regulation
- Consumer behavior change

## 5. Capital Intensity
- High vs low capex model
- Scalability

---

## Key Question
Is this an industry where winners can compound for 5–10 years?
