# Catalyst + Right-Side Entry System

## Core Idea

We do NOT buy bottoms.

We buy:
→ High probability upward acceleration points
→ Supported by catalyst + trend confirmation

---

## Catalyst Types

### 1. Fundamental Catalysts
- Revenue acceleration
- Margin expansion
- Product breakthrough
- New market expansion

---

### 2. Market Catalysts
- Index rotation into sector
- Liquidity expansion
- Rate cut cycle

---

### 3. Narrative Catalysts
- AI adoption wave
- Platform monetization
- Ecosystem expansion

---

### 4. Event Catalysts
- Earnings surprise
- Guidance raise
- Strategic partnership

---

## Right-Side Entry Conditions

A valid entry requires ALL:

### 1. Trend Confirmation
- Weekly trend turns up
- Monthly base formation complete

### 2. Volume Confirmation
- Increasing accumulation volume

### 3. Fundamental Alignment
- Earnings trend stable or accelerating

### 4. Catalyst Present
At least one catalyst identified

---

## Entry Stages

### Stage A: Watch
Downtrend → No action

### Stage B: Base Formation
- Volatility compressing
- No breakdown

### Stage C: Breakout (Preferred Entry)
- Price breaks resistance
- Volume spike
- Catalyst active

---

## Key Principle

Catalyst without trend = trap  
Trend without catalyst = weak trade  
Catalyst + trend = high probability compounder entry
