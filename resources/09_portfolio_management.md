# Portfolio Management

## Portfolio Structure

- Core Compounders (60–80%)
- Growth Bets (10–30%)
- Speculative (0–10%)

---

## Rules

- No single position > 20%
- Rebalance based on thesis change
- Do not average down blindly

---

## Risk Control

- Correlation awareness
- Sector exposure limits
- Macro sensitivity

---

## Key Principle
Survival first, returns second.
