# Macro Cycle Framework

## Key Variables

- Interest rates
- Liquidity conditions
- Inflation trend
- USD strength
- Risk appetite

---

## Cycle Positioning

Identify:
- Expansion phase
- Peak phase
- Contraction phase
- Recovery phase

---

## Impact on Equity

- High growth stocks: most sensitive to liquidity
- Value stocks: more stable
- Defensive sectors: outperform in downturns

---

## Key Principle
Macro does not drive selection, but affects timing and valuation compression/expansion.
