# Research standard

Prefer exchange filings, company reports, regulators, and official policy sources. Record publication date, event date, currency, units, fiscal period, and whether a number is reported, adjusted, estimated, or inferred.

Required coverage: business economics, market and reinvestment runway, moat, management and capital allocation, five-year financial history when available, balance sheet, cash conversion, bear/base/bull cases, implied expectations, catalysts, invalidation, right-side evidence, and a permanent-loss pre-mortem.

Use multiple valuation lenses where appropriate and show sensitivity to growth, margins, multiples, dilution, and FX. Avoid unsupported precision.
