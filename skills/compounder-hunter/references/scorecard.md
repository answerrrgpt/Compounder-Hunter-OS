# Candidate scorecard

Score 0–5 and apply weights: business quality 20, reinvestment runway 15, management/allocation 10, financial quality 10, valuation 20, catalysts 10, right-side confirmation 5, risk/invalidation clarity 10.

- 80–100: priority candidate
- 70–79: core watchlist
- 60–69: research or wait
- below 60: reject or archive

No score overrides a broken thesis, unacceptable permanent-loss risk, unreliable evidence, or excessive valuation.
